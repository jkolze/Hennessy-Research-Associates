Welcome to Hennessy Research Associates


At Hennessy Research Associates we are deeply committed to the research, development and manufacturing of animal health products. Our goal is to provide our clients and the animal health industry in general with high quality, safe, dependable and cost effective products and tools that ensure the health and welfare of animals on a worldwide basis. Our team of highly qualified scientists focuses on delivery of services and guarantee that our rigid standards will more than meet expectations of our animal health industry clients. Dr. Kristina J. Hennessy has over 30 years of progressive, experience in Animal Health research and product development. Attending Kansas State University in Manhattan Kansas, she received her DVM in 1987 and a Ph.D. in 1990. Dr. Hennessy is Board Certified in Immunology with the American College of Veterinary Microbiologists. Throughout her career, Dr. Hennessy led research and product development teams involving all major animal species. Dr. Hennessy also holds several patents towards products for infectious diseases of animals.
